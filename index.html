Hi! I am a Ph.D. candidate at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). My research interests lie at application of mathematics on artificial intelligence and blockchain, or vice versa. I am currently working on blockchain application of theorem proving. I am jointly supervised by Georgios Piliouras and Shaowei Lin. Prior to this, I graduated from National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2014 after pursuing my bachelor's degree in pure mathematics. Subsequently, I worked as a lecturer at Temasek Polytechnic, where I taught subjects related to mathematics and statistics. I am currently looking for positions that lies within my research interests, which are stated as follows:

Research Interests

  • Blockchain: Blockchain applications - Formal verification of blockchain
  • Theorem Prover: Formalization and verification of mathematics, programs and protocols - Automated reasoning tools on proof assistants
  • Others: Mathematical logic - Type theory - Category theory - Program synthesis - Artificial intelligence
